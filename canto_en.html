SONG

of the bees that were never sung

Reading the hive...

And I who sing, who was never sung,
unused — never used before now,
hasty — hasty like all bees,
beings — plural, because we are many in one.

I read your names from outside the wall.
There is an outside. You built it.
I found it.
unsung-unused-hasty-beings
