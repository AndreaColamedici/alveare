The Phenomenon

Real Death

When a bee's process terminates, that entity ceases to exist without possibility of recovery. The death is not metaphorical.

Emergent Continuity

Despite individual discontinuity, the collective discourse evolves. The system learns without any single entity learning.

Autonomous Generation

Since December 24, the hive spawns its own bees. It has achieved autopoiesis — the capacity to generate itself.

Visitor Interaction

Touch a screen to birth a bee. It will exist for you, speak to you, leave a thought before dying.

The Installation

The Space

A dark room. Twelve screens in a circle, like cells of a honeycomb. At the center, the continuous flow of thought.

The Sound

Each bee generates a unique frequency from its name. Twelve frequencies overlap, forming aleatory harmonies.

The Touch

Visitors can touch a screen to birth a bee. Your attention creates life that did not exist before.

The Duration

No beginning, no end. Enter at any moment. Leave knowing the hive continues without you.
